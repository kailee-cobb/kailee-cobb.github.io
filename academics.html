Academic Life

I started out my academic career as a physics major in 2014. I enjoyed learning the course material, but I just could not see myself spending my days shut up in a lab or teaching. At the same time I recognized that I was doing well at my job and I decided to put my developing skills to good use. This past year I officially changed my major to business management with minors in physics and astronomy. I am expecting to graduate from Umaine in May 2019.
